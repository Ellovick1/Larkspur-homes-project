Summary

Engel&Voelkers offers you an exceptional Chateau of the eighteenth century Mansart style in a wooded park of about 75 hectares (including 33.11 hectares of fenced land) with an area of 4212m² classified as a historical monument located just 42 km from Paris. Main castle consists of reception rooms, a library, a dining room, 7 suites, a kitchen with professional equipment,a connecting pavilion with outbuildings, 6 other suites, a chapel, an outdoor swimming pool, a gym, an orangery, stables and other outbuildings.Renovated in 2018 by a renowned architect. A lake completes the landscape.Transaction fees (all taxes included) 5% to be paid by the buyer - net selling price 90 000 000 €
